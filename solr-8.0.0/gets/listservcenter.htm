    Listserv Message Center

    A listserv permits subscribers to send email messages to one another. Normally, membership is required to send or read listserv messages. By special arrangement, however, visitors to this page can read messages posted through the SPN Listserv Message Center (back to February 18, 2005, when the archive was established), and anyone with a Ph.D. related to social psychology can use the button below to post a message to the listservs of these professional societies:

    Society of Experimental Social Psychology
    Society for the Psychological Study of Social Issues

      Note: SPN also distributes any messages posted here to more than 184,000 of its own Twitter and RSS feed subscribers, thereby allowing users to reach a wider audience than the two listservs do.
